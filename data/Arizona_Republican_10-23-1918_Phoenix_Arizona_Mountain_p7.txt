petition the sheriff has pronounced the
following deliverance:

sheriff substitute having considered
the foregoing Petition grants warrant
to cite the within-designed Robert
Thomson Cameron on seven days in
duciae if within Scotland and by edic
tal citation on fourteen days induciae
if forth thereof and grants warrant

John Cameron and James John Cam
eron on fourteen days induciae and

each of the “Glasgow Herald” and
“Phoenix Republican” a notice of the

deliverance and ordains any person
interested if he or they intend to show
cause why the prayer of the petition
should not be granted to lodge in the

a notice of appearance within three
months from the date of the last in
